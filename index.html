    The food option and responsibilities to consider,canteens must be well organised and more efficient than they have been in past. Online menus with photographs of food prepared and their detailed discriptions about the ingrediants help both students and parents make the right choice.Each the students or staff member who access the canteen could be provided with School canteen Account on School website that keeps track of their previous meal choices and any dietary requirments they may have.   • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    1. Accuracy Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with supporting computational devices. Automated attendance management system ensure accurate time records and minimize the inevitable costly errors with manual entry. This accurate data thereby helps to provide accurate performance and payroll data. 2. Economics have you ever thought that attendance management system could control cost? It saves money by putting an end to inaccurate time reporting, buddy punching,time abuse and overpayment. 3. Productivity//Efficiency Monitoring and managing attendance manually can be time-consuming and expensive affair. It takes time to proccess paper sheets and time cards, create schedule and overtime and create payroll manually. Free up precious administration time with an automated system that does it all for you. From keeping track of employee to importing information into your payroll system. The time and effort saved combined data accuracy helps in optimizing the use of resources which lead to increase productivity and improved profit. And many more like visibility/insight.
